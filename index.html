Indian women

kalpana chawla

Kalpana Chawla was the first Indian woman in space. She was born in Karnal, India on March 17, 1962. She earned her degree in aeronautical engineering and immigrated to the United States.

Mother Teresa

Mother Teresa was the first Indian woman to win the Nobel Peace Prize in 1979. She was recognized for her work helping those in need.

Mary Kom

MC Mary Kom was the first Indian woman boxer to win an Olympic medal, when she won bronze in the 51kg category at the 2012 London Olympics. Kom was also the first Indian woman boxer to compete in the Olympics. Before winning her medal, Kom was already a five-time world champion.
